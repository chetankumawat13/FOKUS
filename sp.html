Fokus Hydration - Delivery and Shipping Policy

a) How long will it take for my order to reach me?
We aim to dispatch your order within 24 hours of placement. Orders are
typically delivered within 10-15 days, though unforeseen circumstances may
cause slight delays.

b) Is it possible to track my order?
Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email
with tracking details. You can track your order on the respective delivery
partner's website using your AWB number or mobile number.

c) What are the shipping charges?
For orders below ₹1000, a shipping charge of ₹90 will be applied. Orders
above ₹1000 qualify for free shipping.

d) What time of day will my order be delivered?
Our courier partners will contact you before delivery. Please ensure you are
available to receive their call. If delivery attempts fail three times, the
product will be returned to our warehouse after an email reminder.

e) Do you ship to international locations?
Currently, we only deliver within India.

f) Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
We do not offer Cash on Delivery (COD) at this time.

g) Why is COD not available to me?
Fokus Hydration does not support COD. Please use available online payment
methods to complete your order.
